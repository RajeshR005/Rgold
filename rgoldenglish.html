Understanding Gold Stamping & BIS Hallmark

Over the years, various gold stampings like 22 CT, KDM, 916 KDM, 88 KDM, 22/20 KDM have led to confusion and financial losses for gold sellers. To prevent this, the **Government of India** introduced the **BIS HALLMARK**, ensuring that all gold jewellery meets purity standards. This has eliminated quality concerns, improved trust, and enhanced gold recycling while reducing gold imports.
